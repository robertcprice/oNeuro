🪰 THE FLY
A simulated fruit fly with a brain made of 32 neurons. It uses real neural circuits to smell food and decide where to go.
🍎 FOOD SOURCES
Orange spheres emit odor molecules. The fly's antennae detect these and tell the brain "food nearby!"
💨 MOLECULES
Green particles = food scent molecules floating in the air. More particles = stronger smell. They cluster around food sources.
